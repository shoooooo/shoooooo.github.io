Description

A musical interface built from javascript web audio API

You can move white circle with mouse, and hexagonal blocks under the circle plays tone when you click.

You can play triad chords by covering 3 blocks at once


More Description

Isomorphic Keyboard

It seems like accordion keys, but the layout is different. This layout is built to play triad chords easier.

If you choose any of adjacent three blocks, they will make a major/minor triad.

Just like other isomorphic keyboards, you can change your music's key by moving your hand in parallel.

You can play continuous change of chords by clicking and dragging.

Sound Design

To make all keys sound equal, notes are in Shepard tone.

Bell-shaped filter and its graph for the tone :
y = (0.5+0.5*sin((log(f)-log(130.8))*2*pi/(log(1688)-log(130.8))-pi/2))*1.5*130.8^2/f^2

ADSR envelope :
A = 10ms / D = 700ms / S = 77% / R = 100ms

Sound gain level for each key :
G = a-2*d/3 (d<1.5a)
G = 0 (d>1.5a)
(d is distance between the center of a key and the center of white circle)
(a is the length of side of hexagons)
